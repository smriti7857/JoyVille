Overview

Shapoorji has come up with Joyville Gurugram - an unprecedented residential complex that offers homes with an alluring lifestyle. Its breathtaking floral parks are spread over a 31 acres property with scenic views of greenery that inspire you to make the most of its peerless amenities, every single day. Joyville Gurugram has an impeccable structure with over 24,000 sq.ft. of space and amenities which go beyond the oridnary, the clubhouse allows you to find a place of your own. With apartments powered by JioFiber, the only all-fiber network that gives you services never before experienced in India.
